Vice-Chancellor

Prof. Solomon Adebola

Prof Solomon Ajayi Adebola was born on the 17th of March, 1953, in Kabba. His academic qualifications include a BSc (Hons), Mathematics in 1975 from the University of Ibadan, a Diploma in Management from SW London College in London, an MBA from Southern California University, an MSc (Banking & Finance) from Bayero University, Kano, and a PhD in Management Sciences from University of Ilorin. His professional qualifications include Member Nigerian Institute of Management, Fellow Certified Marketing Communications Institute, Honorary Senior Member Chartered Institute of Bankers of Nigeria, Fellow of the Institute of Taxation of Nigeria, and Fellow of the Institute of Fraud Examiners.

He rose to the peak position of Chief Lecturer in 1992 in Kano State Polytechnic. He later worked as Managing Director of two mortgage banks at different times in Lagos. He was in private practise as Managing Consultant of Starlink Associates, a Management and Tax consulting firm in Lagos.

Since moving back to full academics in the university system, he has taught in Adeleke University Ede, University of Ilorin and Babcock University, among others. He has served at different times as Head of Department and as Dean of Faculty. He is currently an External Examiner to eight (8) universities in Nigeria, and one (1) university in the United Kingdom. He is the immediate past Dean of Babcock Business School, and Director of Babcock Centre for Executive Development. He recently took up a new appointment as the Deputy Vice Chancellor, and Senior Vice President of Adeleke University in Ede. A widely travelled man, Prof Solomon Adebola has also published extensively in reputable national and international journals. A loving family man, his greatest joy is in knowing Jesus Christ as Saviour, and being able to serve in His vineyard.
