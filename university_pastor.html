University Pastor

Pastor Olugbenga Efuntade

Olugbenga Efuntade joined Adeleke University as Assistant University Pastor in 2012. He was appointed as the University Pastor in 2014. He serves in different committees such as the Spiritual Master Plan Committee (chairman), Administrative Committee, Ways and Means Committee, Colloquium Committee, Staff Disciplinary Committee, and Student Disciplinary Committee among others.


Pastor Efuntade was born in Ile-Ife, Osun State. He had his primary and secondary education in Ogun State and obtained a Bachelor of Science Degree in Estate Management (1996/97 session) from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife. His Postgraduate Diploma (PGD) in Pastoral Ministry was from Babcock University, Ilishan Remo. He later obtained a Masters degree in Pastoral Ministry from La Sierra University, California, United States of America. He obtained his Doctor of Theology degree in Missiology from the University of South Africa, Pretoria, South Africa in 2019.


A seasoned Administrator and widely travelled, he was passionately engaged in church ministry while working in the Oil and Gas industry, at the Star-AP Joint Venture. He also worked in the Real Estate industry and other service sectors before he finally heeded the call to fulltime ministry in 2007. He served as an Assistant Pastor at Victory Sanctuary Seventh Day Adventist Church, Lekki, Lagos and has had the privilege of working and ministering in different fora within and outside Nigeria. Olugbenga Efuntade has facilitated seminars on marriage, community organizing/social justice and discovering purpose. He served as a member of faculty at the Victory Sanctuary Leadership Training Institute. He has pastored and mentored teenagers/young adults and has been invited severally as a speaker, facilitator, presenter or counsellor in seminars involving personal growth and development. He is passionate about impacting knowledge and growing leaders. Olugbenga Efuntade is an ardent believer of social justice and maximizing potential.


He has presented papers both locally and internationally, authored books, published papers and contributed to anthologies.


He is happily married to Gbonjubola and they are blessed with two wonderful children.
